## Context

Systems are often understood in terms of activity.

But activity alone does not explain behavior.

---

## Model

Flow describes what moves through a system.

Accumulation describes what builds when movement is imbalanced.

When inflow exceeds outflow, accumulation forms.

---

## Breakdown

1. Resources enter the system  
2. Resources exit the system  
3. If inflow > outflow → accumulation builds  
4. Accumulation increases delay and complexity  

---

## Insight

Flow explains activity.

Accumulation explains behavior.
