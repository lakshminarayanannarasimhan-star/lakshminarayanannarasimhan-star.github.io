## Context

Growth is often expected to improve performance.

But as systems expand, they frequently become slower and less efficient.

---

## Model

Growth increases inflow into the system.

Capacity does not increase at the same rate.

As flow approaches limits, delays increase and accumulation builds.

---

## Breakdown

1. Inflow increases with growth  
2. Capacity remains limited  
3. Flow approaches system limits  
4. Delays increase  
5. Accumulation builds  

---

## Insight

The system is not failing.

It is reaching its constraints.

Growth exposes structure.
