## Context

Most problem solving focuses on improving individual parts of a system.

Better tools, faster execution, increased effort.

Yet outcomes often remain unchanged.

---

## Model

A system is regulated by its constraints.

Improvements outside the constraint do not change system output.

---

## Breakdown

1. Effort is applied across multiple parts  
2. The constraint remains unchanged  
3. Flow is still restricted at the same point  
4. System output does not improve  

---

## Insight

A system improves only when its constraint changes.

Everything else is secondary.
