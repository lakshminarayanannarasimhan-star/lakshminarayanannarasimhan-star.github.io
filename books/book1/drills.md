## Chapter 1 — Flow and Movement

### Recognition
- Identify activity vs movement  
- Detect accumulation in a system  
- Recognize uneven movement  

### Structuring
- Map stages of work progression  
- Identify where movement slows  
- Trace flow across the system  

### Interpretation
- Explain why accumulation forms  
- Distinguish activity from completion  
- Identify early signals of constrained movement  

### Decision
- Identify where intervention is needed  
- Evaluate impact of increasing activity  
- Decide where to focus attention
