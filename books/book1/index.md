# 📘 The Indispensable Position
### *How You See*

---

## 🧠 What This Book Does

This book builds the ability to **see systems clearly**.

Most people operate at the level of events.  
They react, fix symptoms, and repeat mistakes.

This book trains you to:

- See structure beneath events  
- Identify constraints and bottlenecks  
- Understand how systems behave  
- Make high-quality decisions under uncertainty  

---

## 🎯 Core Capability

- System Thinking  
- Constraint Identification  
- Mechanism Clarity  
- Decision Quality
